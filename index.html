About Us

Engineering Excellence in Thermal Solutions

Established in 2006 by UET Lahore 1st-class engineer Ijaz Goraya, Boiler Techniques delivers premier thermal engineering solutions. Our highly qualified team brings top-tier academic training and field expertise from Pakistan’s most prestigious institutions to provide safe, eco-friendly heating designs.

  • Elite Engineering Team: Certified experts from UET, NUST, and COMSATS.
  • Eco-Friendly Solutions: Engineered for optimal efficiency and sustainability.
  • Uncompromised Safety: Strict compliance protocols and precision diagnostics.
